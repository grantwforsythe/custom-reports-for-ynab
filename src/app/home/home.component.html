I officially became a YNABer back in 2024 and have been hooked ever since. I believe YNAB to be a great product; however, I find the reports feature to be lacking even with the Toolkit for YNAB which is why I started this project. I hope others will deprive some use from it as well.

To get started, click the 'Authenticate' button on the top right-hand corner of your screen.

For those that want to contribute or report a bug, please do so from the GitHub repository linked below.

Please read the privacy policy to learn how your data is being accessed. TL;DR: All of your data is stored locally in your browser and will be cleared once you close it.
