Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2024

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our site, app, and services (refer to as the "App" from here on), and the choices you have associated with that data.

YNAB Data and OAuth

The App follows Terms of Service for the YNAB API

  • The data obtained through the YNAB API is never stored on an application server. The authorization token is stored in localstorage within your browser. All data processing is done locally on your browser.
  • Other than YNAB's own servers, this app does not communicate with a backend server.
  • The application will never ask for any financial account credentials.

We collect

We do not collect any information from your YNAB account.

Data Removal

When you logout of the App, all your YNAB data and the App's own preferences are cleared from your browser. Your data is not stored anywhere outside of your own browser.

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy from time to time.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
